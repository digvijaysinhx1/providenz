Typical work a creative web agency does:

• Brand identity (logo, color system, typography)
• High-end website UI design
• Custom animations and interactions
• UX strategy (how users move through the site)
• Visual storytelling for the brand
• Marketing-focused landing pages
• Sometimes 3D, motion graphics, WebGL, etc.

Think of the difference like this:

A normal website agency builds a restaurant website with menu, contact page, gallery.

A creative web agency builds a site where
the hero section animates, the story scrolls visually, the typography feels premium, and the brand feels like an experience.

Companies hire them when they want brand impact, not just an online presence.
